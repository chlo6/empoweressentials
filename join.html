Open Positions

Treasurer

Tracks funds raised by members via Google Sheets and works with inventory manager and fundraising coordinator to plan budgeting.

Inventory Manager

Tracks kits numbers through Google Sheets, ensures quality, and works with treasurer for budgeting and ordering.

Fundraising Coordinator

Tracks donations given to organization, organizes fundraising events, and work with treasurer to align fundraising with budget needs.

Outreach Coordinator

Connects with local businesses for donations (including kit materials!), handles external email inbox, and works with President and VP for recruiting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time commitment is required?

We meet for about 3 hours monthly. Additional time varies based on your role and events.

Do I need any prior experience?

No experience necessary! We welcome everyone who wants to make a difference.

Can I volunteer without being an officer?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers at all our events and kit-building sessions.
